About Me

My name is Nathan DeJarnette, and I am an Audio/Visual specialist with a passion for creating stunning visual and audio content. With years of experience under my belt, I provide a wide range of services that include videography, video editing, audio editing, voiceover work, foley, and audio for video. In addition to these services, I also offer event services such as live streaming, multi-camera, event coordination, event/wedding photography and videography, DJ services, audio/visual equipment and operators. Whether you're looking to create a promotional video for your business or capture the special moments of your wedding day, I am dedicated to delivering high-quality results that exceed your expectations.
